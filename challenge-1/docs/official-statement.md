# Challenge 1 Official Statement

## Title

AI Agent for Rescheduling Housing Arrears

## Concerned Department

Finance and Collection Department, in coordination with the Information Technology Department.

## Problem Statement

The challenge focuses on developing a specialized AI agent to instantly assess rescheduling requests by analyzing the application data and supporting documents, verifying the completeness of requirements, evaluating the beneficiary's situation, and then proposing the appropriate rescheduling amount and duration in accordance with approved governance rules and requirements.

This will support the immediate and standardized delivery of the service without the need to go through the full current manual assessment cycle.

## Expected Impact

The challenge will contribute to transforming the rescheduling service from a process that currently takes an average of five working days into an instant service.

This will be achieved by streamlining and, where possible, eliminating internal processes and procedures, while reducing manual intervention in the assessment of repetitive or straightforward cases.

The solution will also enhance transparency and governance in determining the rescheduling amount and duration by applying unified rules to all cases, automatically documenting the rationale behind each decision, and ensuring consistency in processing and fairness in evaluation.

Exceptional or high-risk cases will remain subject to referral to the concerned employee when needed.

## Available Data

The prototype can be developed using synthetic data or non-sensitive samples, including:

- rescheduling request data
- arrears amount
- duration of delay
- income and financial obligations data
- required supporting documents
- reasons for requesting rescheduling
- approved controls and requirements
- rules for determining the rescheduling amount and duration

Later, previous case data may be used after proper processing and anonymization of personal information, with the aim of improving classification and recommendation accuracy.
