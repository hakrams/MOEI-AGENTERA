# Challenge 2 Official Statement

## Title

AI-Powered Global Country Intelligence Platform

## Arabic Title

منصة الذكاء الاصطناعي للملفات الدولية والذكاء الاستراتيجي

## Problem Statement

Government leadership teams often require rapid access to reliable and consolidated information about countries, international entities, and strategic sectors related to energy and infrastructure.

Currently, gathering such information is time-consuming, fragmented across multiple sources, and highly dependent on manual preparation, which may impact meeting readiness, decision-making speed, and strategic collaboration opportunities during international engagements.

## Challenge

Implementing the following capabilities:

- Develop an AI-powered executive intelligence platform that provides centralized and interactive country profiles related to energy, infrastructure, transport, sustainability, and economic sectors.
- Enable AI-generated country summaries, including strategic overviews, government structure, key initiatives, partnerships, national priorities, major projects, and bilateral cooperation opportunities.
- Provide real-time access to critical indicators and statistics such as GDP, energy production, infrastructure indexes, trade indicators, sustainability metrics, smart city initiatives, and competitiveness rankings.
- Automatically generate executive-ready briefing materials, including concise presentation slides, executive summaries, talking points, and smart reports for leadership meetings and international visits.
- Generate AI-powered short videos and visual summaries about countries, projects, achievements, and strategic opportunities to support leadership engagement and presentations.
- Enable intelligent search and conversational AI capabilities to allow leadership teams to quickly retrieve information, compare countries, identify collaboration opportunities, and access strategic insights in seconds.
- Integrate trusted global data sources, international reports, government publications, and analytics platforms to ensure information accuracy, reliability, and continuous updates.
- Provide predictive and analytical insights to support strategic partnerships, investment opportunities, benchmarking, and international decision-making.

## Success Looks Like

An AI-powered strategic intelligence platform that enables leadership teams to instantly access accurate, interactive, and executive-level country intelligence, improving preparedness for international meetings, accelerating strategic decision-making, enhancing collaboration opportunities, and strengthening the UAE's global engagement in energy and infrastructure sectors.
