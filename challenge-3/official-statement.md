# Challenge 3 Official Statement

## Title

Omnichannel AI Customer Engagement Agent

## Arabic Title

وكيل التفاعل الذكي متعدد القنوات مع المتعاملين

## Problem Statement

MOEI engages with customers across multiple channels -- WhatsApp, the contact center voice channel, and the website/mobile application -- but each channel currently operates in isolation, with limited AI capabilities, manual dependencies, and fragmented operational visibility.

This results in:

- delayed responses
- inconsistent service quality across channels
- repeated customer effort, where customers re-explain the same issue on each channel
- limited personalization
- reactive rather than proactive engagement

Leadership lacks a unified, real-time view of customer experience and operational performance across all touchpoints.

## Challenge

Build an Agentic AI system that provides a unified, intelligent customer engagement layer across all three channels:

- WhatsApp
- Voice Contact Center
- Website and Mobile Application

## A. Conversational Intelligence -- Cross-Channel

- Natural Language Understanding in Arabic and English with consistent intent recognition, shared knowledge base, and persistent customer context across channels.
- Generative AI-powered responses for inquiries, complaints, suggestions, and appreciations, with seamless handover between channels. Example: start a request on WhatsApp, complete on the mobile app.
- Real-time voice tone and sentiment analysis on calls, including stress, anger, and satisfaction, feeding the same unified customer profile used in chat and the app.

## B. Operational Execution

- Automated case creation, tracking, routing, and resolution integrated with CRM and backend government services.
- Instant updates, smart notifications, and proactive follow-ups via the customer's preferred channel.
- Personalized service guidance, next-best-action recommendations, and intelligent self-service based on customer history and behavior.
- AI-powered smart search across services, policies, FAQs, and procedures, available consistently on web, mobile, and chat.

## C. Agent and Leadership Enablement

- AI co-pilot for human contact-center agents, including next-best-action, automated knowledge support, decision support, and real-time coaching.
- Unified executive dashboard with real-time KPIs, sentiment trends, channel-level performance, escalation risks, and workforce productivity.
- Predictive analytics for call/chat volume forecasting, demand patterns, escalation risks, and proactive workforce planning.

## Success Looks Like

A working prototype demonstrating a single, unified AI customer engagement agent operating across WhatsApp, the voice contact center, and the website/mobile application, with measurable improvements in:

- time-to-resolution
- first-contact resolution rate
- customer effort score
- CSAT
- self-service deflection

Leadership has one real-time dashboard providing full omnichannel visibility, and human agents have a working AI co-pilot.

- operational KPIs
