Sah Labs OneCX Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 8, 2026

Sah Labs OneCX is a hackathon prototype created to demonstrate omnichannel customer support and service orchestration. It is not an official Ministry of Energy and Infrastructure service, and it does not issue official government decisions.

Information We May Collect

During testing, the prototype may collect information entered or sent by test users, including names, phone numbers, customer identifiers, WhatsApp messages, website chat messages, call transcript text, service status references, uploaded-test-document metadata, interaction events, and operational logs.

How We Use Information

We use test information only to demonstrate customer support flows, cross-channel continuity, service-status assistance, officer handoff, dashboard reporting, and AI-assisted response generation. The prototype must not be used to make final official approvals, rejections, financial decisions, or binding government commitments.

Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Test data may be processed by the prototype server and configured service providers needed for testing, such as messaging, hosting, or AI API providers. Data should be treated as test/demo information unless a formal production agreement is established.

Data Retention

Test data is retained only as needed for hackathon testing, troubleshooting, and demonstration. It may be deleted during resets, cleanup, or upon request.

User Control and Deletion

Users may request deletion of prototype test data by following the instructions at /data-deletion/ or by contacting akram@sahlabs.me.

Contact

For privacy questions about this prototype, contact akram@sahlabs.me.
